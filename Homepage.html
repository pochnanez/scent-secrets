Dior Sauvage Eau de Parfum (EDP) is a captivating, intense, and refined fragrance for men, launched in 2018 as a richer interpretation of the original Sauvage. It builds on the raw freshness of its predecessor, offering a more complex and enveloping scent profile with oriental and vanilla facets.

The fragrance opens with a vibrant burst of fresh, spicy Calabrian bergamot and pepper. This leads into an aromatic heart featuring lavender, star anise, and nutmeg, adding warmth and depth. The base settles into a smooth, sensual, and earthy finish with notes of ambroxan, vanilla, and patchouli.
