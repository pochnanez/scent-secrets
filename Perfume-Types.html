PERFUME TYPES

Discover the different types of fragrances categorized by concentration and longevity. Understanding each type helps you choose the right scent for your lifestyle, preferences, and occasions. From light and refreshing mists to rich and long-lasting parfums, explore the world of fragrance and find your perfect match.

Parfum / Extrait +
Parfum, also known as Extrait, has the highest fragrance concentration among all types. It is very rich and intense, usually lasting more than 8 hours on the skin. Because of its strength, only a small amount is needed for all-day wear.
Eau de Parfum (EDP) +
Eau de Parfum has a high concentration of fragrance oils, making it strong and long-lasting. It typically stays on the skin for about 6-8 hours. This type is ideal for evening wear or special occasions.
Eau de Toilette (EDT) +
Eau de Toilette is lighter and fresher compared to EDP. It usually lasts around 3-5 hours, making it suitable for daily or casual use. It gives a noticeable scent without being too overpowering.
Eau de Cologne (EDC) +
Eau de Cologne has a lower fragrance concentration, making it light and refreshing. It typically lasts 2-3 hours on the skin. This type is perfect for hot weather or quick reapplication throughout the day.
Body Mist / Spray +
Body mists have the lightest concentration among all fragrance types. They provide a subtle scent that usually lasts 1-2 hours. They are ideal for a quick refresh or layering with other fragrances.
